About the Property

Nestled into the hillside, this move-in-ready, newly built (2024) 3-bedroom, 2-bath condo offers comfort, connection, and a vibrant sense of play. As part of Treehouse Village Ecohousing, Atlantic Canada's first cohousing community, this home was built to the rigorous Passive House standard, keeping it naturally cool in summer and warm in winter.

The open floor plan has made this home ideal for our family of four—we've hosted countless kids' playdates, potlucks, and parties. Abundant natural light fills the space throughout the day, while the master bedroom offers peaceful nights with two exterior walls that share no neighbours. Located on the ground floor with full wheelchair accessibility, the kitchen and bathrooms are thoughtfully designed with mobility in mind.
