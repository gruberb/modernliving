Living Lightly, Connected Deeply at Treehouse Village

Nestled into the hillside, this move-in-ready, newly built (2024) 3-bedroom, 2-bath condo (1269 sq. ft.) offers comfort, connection, and a vibrant sense of play. As part of Treehouse Village Ecohousing, Atlantic Canada's first cohousing community, this home was built to the rigorous Passive House standard, keeping it naturally cool in summer and warm in winter.

The spacious living area features beautiful Revwood Premier laminate flooring in Fresh Spring Pecan throughout and has plenty of room for both work and leisure. The kitchen boasts solid surface countertops in Silver/White Sand. The kitchen and second bedroom catch the morning sun and overlook the gardens and shared alcove porch. Here, neighbours cross paths easily—and sometimes enjoy the scent of fresh baking drifting from next door. Just beyond is the car-free garden courtyard, a safe, lively space where children play, tables are pulled together for casual outdoor meals, and friendships flourish.

The open floor plan has made this home ideal for our family of four—we've hosted countless kids' playdates, potlucks, and parties over the years. Abundant natural light fills the space throughout the day, while the master bedroom offers peaceful nights with two exterior walls that share no neighbours. Located on the ground floor with full wheelchair accessibility, the kitchen and bathrooms are thoughtfully designed with mobility in mind.

Condo fees ($477/month) include heat, electricity, water, and internet, plus access to inspiring shared amenities: a dining hall and kitchen, workshop, coworking and flex rooms, gym, guest suites, forest, gardens, and more. Sewage is approximately $500/year (separate). The 16-acre property connects directly to the Centennial Trail, with shops, schools, and recreation all within walking distance. On-site EV car sharing means you can book a vehicle only when you need one.

Over $5,600 in Quality Appliances Included

Haier counter-depth 4-door refrigerator, GE Profile range, GE dishwasher, and Midea 24" washer & dryer—all included.

The sellers are open to negotiate a rent agreement if you want to purchase this property as a future home or current investment.

Treehouse Village is a place where living lightly, joyfully, and comfortably all come together. It's more than a condo—it's an invitation to belong.

Bridgewater &
the South Shore

Bridgewater is a thriving, progressive town in the heart of the scenic South Shore of Nova Scotia – close to stunning white sand beaches, parks, and trails, and with many amenities including a regional hospital.

  • • Treehouse Village connects directly to the accessible Centennial Trail, with shops, schools, and recreation all within walking/biking distance.
  • • Over 125 kms of trails connect the Centennial Trail to Mahone Bay, Lunenburg and beyond
  • • The UNESCO World Heritage Site of Old Town Lunenburg, is home of the Bluenose II
  • • Over 20 white-sand ocean beaches within a 1-hour drive (Crescent Beach, Hirtle's Beach, ...)
  • • Public schools in English or French, Waldorf school, Community College
  • • See the Bridgewater and surrounding Lunenburg County explore sites for more information
